Travel to Koh Rong

Introducing Koh Rong:

Koh rong is a amazing place to travel to if you want to see the ocean where ever you go. Koh rong is an island at the edge of Cambodia. The island has clear blue water and unique sand colours. there are coral reefs and waterfalls there. It takes around 45 minutes to get there. The most amazing sight to see there is the glowing planktons which you can only see late at night by travelling by a small boat to the other side of the island.

Things to do in Koh Rong:

  • Boat trip around the island
  • Zipline through the jungle
  • go snorkeling
  • See the glowing planktons
